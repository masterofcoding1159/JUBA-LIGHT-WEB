About Juba Light Organization

Juba Light Organization

Empowering vulnerable communities in Somalia through sustainable development, peacebuilding, and resilience programs since 2011.

Our Vision

To build thriving, resilient societies where poverty is eradicated, education is revitalized, and every individual enjoys dignity, food security, and equal opportunity.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities, promote sustainable livelihoods, and advance peace and equitable development through transparency, accountability, and inclusive engagement.
